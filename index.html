William Henry Gates III

The genius behind Microsoft

Bill Gates timeline:


  • 1955 - Born in Seattle, WA
  • 1968 - Enrolled in Lakeside Prep School and wrote first software program
  • 1972 - Gates formed a venture called Traf-O-Data with childhood friend Paul Allen
  • 1973 - Graduated from Lakeside School
  • 1975 - Launched Microsoft with Paul Allen as co-owners
  • 1980 - IBM approached Microsoft about writing a BASIC interpreter
  • 1985 - Microsoft launched its first retail of Microsoft Windows
  • 1986 - Struck a deal to develop a seperate operating system with IBM
  • 1994 - Marries Malinda Gates
  • 2006 - Gates announced that he would transition out of his role at Microsoft to dedicate more time to philanthropy. He divided his responsibilities between two successors when he placed Ray Ozzie in charge of management and Craig Mundie in charge of long-term product strategy.
  • 2013 - Became the highest earning billionaire according to Bloomberg Billionaire Index
  • 2019 - Networth value of 105.5 billion
